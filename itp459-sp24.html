ITP 459 : Generative AI and Applied Machine Learning

for Natural Language Processing (NLP)

University of Southern California / Spring 2024

This course provides an in-depth exploration of Artificial Intelligence technologies, with a particular focus on advancements in Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Generative AI. Delve into renowned AI models like GPT, BERT, and their applications, gaining hands-on experience with powerful tools like TensorFlow, Keras, and Hugging Face.

Throughout the course, you'll gain a solid understanding of NLP fundamentals and learn about the diverse range of technologies and products developed using NLP and Generative AI. This includes a comprehensive study of how these technologies are revolutionizing text processing and generation. Key highlights of the course include practical sessions on utilizing pre-trained Large Language Models (LLMs), where you'll learn how to use their APIs for various applications. You'll also acquire skills in fine-tuning LLMs for specialized tasks, and gain insights into the methodologies for retraining these models to suit specific requirements.

AI Tools Policy

Students are required to independently submit their solutions for ITP 459 homework assignments. Collaboration with generative AI tools such as Co-Pilot and ChatGPT is allowed, treating them as collaborators in the problem-solving process. However, the direct solicitation of answers or copying solutions, whether from peers or external sources, is strictly prohibited.
